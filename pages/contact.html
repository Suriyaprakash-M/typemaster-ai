Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TypeMaster AI really free? +
Yes! Our core typing test, basic analytics, and 3 typing modes are free forever — no credit card required. We offer a Pro plan for users who want the full AI Coach, all 12 modes, and advanced analytics.
How does the AI Typing Coach actually work? +
Our AI analyses keystroke timing, error patterns, and finger-level performance from every test you take. It then identifies your specific weaknesses — like hesitation on certain letters — and generates a personalised practice plan targeting exactly those issues.
Can I use TypeMaster AI for placement test preparation? +
Absolutely. Our Placement Prep Mode includes real aptitude questions, technical definitions, and HR interview answer content — specifically designed for campus recruitment typing tests used by companies like TCS, Infosys, and Wipro.
Does TypeMaster AI support Tamil typing? +
Yes, we support full Tamil typing with phonetic input, alongside English. You can switch languages mid-session and practice with dedicated Tamil literature and exam-style content.
How do I cancel my Pro subscription? +
You can cancel anytime from your Profile → Settings → Billing page. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your current billing cycle, and we don't ask any retention questions.
Can my school or college get a bulk plan? +
Yes! Our Team plan supports up to 50 members with an admin dashboard, progress reports, and custom content. For larger institutions, email partnerships@typemasterai.com for custom pricing.
