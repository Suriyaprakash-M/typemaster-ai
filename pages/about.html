Our Mission

Typing speed shouldn't be
a barrier to opportunity

Every year, millions of students lose placement opportunities, programmers lose productivity hours, and professionals lose confidence — all because nobody ever taught them to type properly.

We built TypeMaster AI to close that gap with real AI coaching, not just a stopwatch and a paragraph. Our mission is to make world-class typing education free, fun, and accessible to everyone — starting in India and expanding globally.

Our Journey

From Idea to 500K Users

🚀
2023
TypeMaster AI is Founded
Started as a college project in Chennai to help engineering students prepare for placement typing tests.
💻
2024 Q1
Coding Typing Mode Launches
First platform to offer dedicated typing practice for Python, Java, C, and SQL — reaching 50,000 users in 3 months.
🌏
2024 Q3
Tamil Typing Support Added
Became the first major typing platform with native Tamil + English bilingual switching.
🤖

Launched our proprietary mistake-analysis engine, personalising training for every single user.
🏆

Crossed half a million learners across 40 countries, with the Battle Arena multiplayer mode launching the same quarter.
